Code Generator

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Overview

Code Generator ("we", "our", or "the service") is a free tool that converts Figma designs into production-ready code. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate the service:

  • Figma URLs — entered by you to generate code. These are processed in real time and not stored on our servers.
  • Figma Access Token — stored only in your browser's localStorage. We never transmit or store it on our servers beyond the API call you initiate.
  • Usage analytics — anonymous page-view data (via Google Analytics, if enabled) including browser type, country, and session duration. No personally identifiable information is collected.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Google AdSense ad serving and measurement
  • Anonymous analytics (Google Analytics, if enabled)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. This will not affect the core code-generation functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

When you generate code, your Figma URL is sent to the Figma API on your behalf. We also route requests through our serverless API to call an AI provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, Groq, or Google Gemini) for code generation. These calls include design data extracted from your Figma file. Please review those providers' privacy policies for details on their data handling.

6. Data Retention

We do not store Figma URLs, tokens, or generated code on our servers. All processing is ephemeral — once the API call completes, no data is retained.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
